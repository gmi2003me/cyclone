Cyclone Arcade Game

Welcome!

Get ready to test your timing and reflexes in this web-based version of the classic arcade game, Cyclone!

How to Play:

  • Lights will circle around the game board.
  • Each light has a point value. Higher points mean a smaller scoring window!
  • Player 1 (Red): Press Left Shift or the Red Button.
  • Player 2 (Green): Press Space Bar or the Green Button.
  • Player 3 (Blue): Press Enter/Return or the Blue Button.
  • Press your button when the moving light is over a point light you want to hit.
  • You must press within the specific time window for that light to score. The window is shorter for higher-value lights.
  • Each player has 10 attempts. Highest score wins!
